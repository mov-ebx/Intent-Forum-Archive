      For any players hacking on Hypixel I have a short guide on avoiding a ban.

      • Don't blatantly hack on skywars as this will get you banned. Scaffolding to kill everyone with high reach will get you banned in a few games. Through my experience skywars is closely monitored by staff members and if you play with a friend who gets banned watch out for the type of message

      Types of messages

      • "A player has been removed from your game for hacking or abuse" means a player was manually banned by a staff member. Often the staff member will stay in the game and watch the team mate of the banned person and continue watching the rest of the game. Expect to be watched for several games, so I suggest you log out for 10 minutes

      "[WATCH DOG] A player has been removed from your game for hacking or abuse" this message indicates the player who was banned was banned by watch dog and any other hackers in the game don't have to worry about being watched.

      • Hacking blatantly in 40 vs 40 bedwars is High risk you can and most likely will be recorded and caught.

      Risks

      • Skywars: High Risk

      • Bedwars 4 vs 4 vs 4 vs 4: Moderate Risk

      • Bedwars Solo: Low Risk

      • Bedwars Doubles: Low Risk

      • Bedwars 4 vs 4: Low Risk
