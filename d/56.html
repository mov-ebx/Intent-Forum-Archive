      Proxies in Zeroday and Minecraft in general aren’t widely used and can be extremely confusing to troubleshoot at first so I thought I’d make a guide. If you have the choice between a proxy and a VPN use a VPN. Proxies are really for those of us that can’t use a VPN for whatever reason.

      1. Get a socks 4/5/HTTP proxy. I just searched socks 5 proxy on google and got a bunch of free ones to use. Make sure to use one that says anonymous or IP hide ETC. Most sites tell you the different levels you can find on their site.
      2. Input the proxy number that’s longer (the IP). Then put a : and type the shorter numbers (the port). An IP normally looks something like this: xxx.x.xx.x and a port normally looks something like this: XXXX
      3. go to alt manager and right click the alt you want to use and press “new session”
      4. join a server, you will most likely have higher ping but you won’t be IP banned if you use a good proxy.

      Tips:
      Pick a non American proxy. Proxies that are from the U.S. tend to be caught by Anti-VPNs more often than non American proxies.

      Test you proxy by going on a server such as mineplex that prevents you from joining if you have a proxy or VPN but won’t band your account.

      Make sure you use a proxy that has fast speeds. If you don't you will be unable to play because of the lag.
